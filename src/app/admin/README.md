## Admin ##

The admin is the one responsible for managing the issues in their specific community or baranggay. It could be LGU's, barangay council, or any other official responsible for handling issues within their area. They are directly under the Cebu City Government.

The admins are tasked to delegate tasks to workforces in their municipality. They are also responsible for monitoring the progress of the workforces and ensuring that the issues are resolved successfully and on time.
