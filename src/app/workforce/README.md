## Workforce ##

This is for future implementation of the workforces in the system. They are the ones who are responsible for handling the issues reported by the clients. They will be assigned by the admin based on the type of issue reported.

Example workforces:
- Street Sweeper
- Garbage Collector
- Road Repair Team
- Drainage Maintenance Team
- Public Safety Team
- Philippine National Police
- Firefighters
- Medical Personnels
- Sangguniang Kabataan

The workforces are directly under the admin's supervision. They are responsible for executing the tasks delegated by the admin and ensuring that the issues are resolved successfully and on time.
